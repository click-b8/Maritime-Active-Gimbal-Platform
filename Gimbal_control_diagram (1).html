Control Law
u(t) = α · θ̈_FF (IMU 2)  +  (1 − α) · u_PID (IMU 1)
α → 1: full feedforward — fast, proactive, model-dependent
α → 0: full PID feedback — robust, corrective, reactive
Plant: I·θ̈ + c·θ̇ + k·θ = T
Tuning note: IMU 2 sees hull disturbances before they reach the platform — giving the feedforward path a timing advantage over pure feedback. High α exploits this anticipation. If the inverse model C⁻¹ is inaccurate (e.g., under nonlinear sea states), reduce α to let the PID correct residual error from IMU 1.
